Houses & Lore

The great families whose rivalries shaped a continent.

House Stark

The North

House Stark

"Winter is Coming"

Lords of Winterfell for thousands of years. Their words are not a boast — they are a warning. Defined by honour, duty, and a deep bond with the land they protect.

Key Members

  • Ned Stark — Lord of Winterfell, Hand of the King
  • Robb Stark — King in the North
  • Jon Snow — Lord Commander, King in the North
  • Sansa Stark — Queen in the North
  • Arya Stark — Faceless assassin
  • Bran Stark — The Three-Eyed Raven

House Lannister

The Westerlands

House Lannister

"Hear Me Roar" / "A Lannister Always Pays His Debts"

The wealthiest family in Westeros. Their gold has bought thrones, silence, and wars. Power built on ruthlessness and fear.

Key Members

  • Tywin Lannister — Lord of Casterly Rock, patriarch
  • Cersei Lannister — Queen of the Seven Kingdoms
  • Jaime Lannister — The Kingslayer
  • Tyrion Lannister — The Imp, Hand of the Queen

House Targaryen

Dragonstone

House Targaryen

"Fire and Blood"

For nearly three centuries, the Targaryens ruled with dragons. When the dragons died, the dynasty rotted from within until Robert's Rebellion ended their reign.

Key Members

  • Aerys II — The Mad King
  • Rhaegar Targaryen — Crown Prince, father of Jon Snow
  • Daenerys Targaryen — Mother of Dragons
  • Jon Snow — Born Aegon Targaryen, true heir

House Baratheon

The Stormlands

House Baratheon

"Ours is the Fury"

Founded on rebellion when Robert overthrew the Mad King. Fractured after his death and nearly destroyed by the War of the Five Kings.

Key Members

  • Robert Baratheon — King of the Seven Kingdoms
  • Stannis Baratheon — Lord of Dragonstone
  • Renly Baratheon — Lord of Storm's End
  • Joffrey Baratheon — King, Robert's supposed heir
